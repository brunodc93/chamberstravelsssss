Rome

PLACES TO VISIT, ROME, VATICAN CITY, ASSISI, LANCIANO, SAN GIOVANNI ROTONDO

DAY 10 ROME-USA

After breakfast to the airport and return to USA…Thanks for being part of our Pilgrimage God bless..

DAY 09 ROME

This day will be on our own. have fun! be ready for departure in the morning …pack our luggage
Dinner and overnight will be at the hotel

DAY 08 SAN GIOVANNI ROTONDO-ROME

We will have some free time in San Giovanni Rotondo then return to Rome dinner and
overnight at hotel

DAY 06/7 LANCIANO-SAN GIOVANNI ROTONDO

Depending on the time of arrival, we may have our own walking tour in Rotondo. We will
visit the church where Padre Pio celebrated Mass and heard confessions. Padre PioHad the gift of discernment and would often reveal what was not confessed by the penitent. Thishelped the penitent to make a good confession and be at peace. Then we visit
the mount of San Angelo. We will have then personal prayer time and activities such as
shopping. Diner and overnight at hotel

DAY 05 ASSISI LANCIANO

We will depart to Lanciano visit the church where the miracle of the Eucharist took place.
A priest came to Lanciano, to celebrate Mass and when he elevated the Eucharist,
human blood was on it and still is. After our visit and confirm the Miracle we will
continue our journey to San Giovanni Rotondo

DAY 04 ROME-FLORENCE

We will visit Siena, were we will stroll the Piaza del campo one of the finest square in Italy, Then we
will visit the church of St. Mary of the Angels. We will continue to Assisi one of the must famous
in the world, at St. Francis of Assisi. The rest of the day will be in our own for personal prayers
and or activities. Dinner and overnight at hotel

DAY 03 VATICAN CITY PAPAL AUDIENCE

After our early breakfast we will join pilgrims from around the world at Vatican City in the weekly “
Papal Audience and Blessing” (subject to the Pope’s health and schedule). After lunch (on our
own) , we will visit the Museum and the famous Sistine Chapel painted by Michael Angelodinner and overnight at hotel

DAY 02 ROME THE ETERNAL CITY

Meet with the tour guide and our transportation at Rome’s Airport, transfer to the hotel
after check inn at the hotel we will get ready for our city tour. We will visit St. Major Basilica, St.
Paul out of the walls, and the coliseum. We will have some free time on our own visit some famous
Places, dinner and overnight at the hotel

DAY 01 HOUSTON-ROME

Depart Houston and enjoy a dinner served onboard in our overnight flight

Rome is one of the places you would like to visit pilgrims from all over the world gather to visit the beautiful Rome to se the Pope and to see the history of such place, usually the Papal Audience take place on Wednesdays (if he is available) in the Vatican City, The Vatican you need to see to believe it is the most beautiful Vatican in the world, the same with the Sisteen Chapel painted by Michael Angelo.
